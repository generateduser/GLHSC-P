How Things Work

The Green Level non-competitive coding & programming club primarily focuses on (as the name suggests), the non-competitive aspect of coding, which mainly includes learning and sharpening skills in various coding languages, collabing on & uploading projects, and hosting mock competitions. If you want to get become a better coder and build real-world experience, this club is for you!

If you are interested in learning more about the club, or want to join, feel free to sign up on AllTimely! You can come join us at any time of the year; no membership necessary. If you have any questions, email Mr. Fox or one of our officers.
